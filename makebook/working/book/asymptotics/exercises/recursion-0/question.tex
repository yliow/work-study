You are given the following recursive function:
\[
f(n) = 
\begin{cases}
2           & \text{if n = 0} \\
3f(n-1) + 5 &\text{otherwise}
\end{cases}
\]
Compute $f(7)$ performing your computation like a computer,
evaluating one operation of performing only one substitution at a time.
