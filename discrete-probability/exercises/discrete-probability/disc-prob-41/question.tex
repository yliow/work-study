  Suppose you make $n$ rolls of a fair die.
  What is the sum of the consecutive repeats?
  For instance suppose you roll
  $1, 4, \underline{3, 3}, 2, \underline{5, 5, 5}, 1$.
  The sum of consecutive repeats is $3 + 3 + 5 + 5 + 5$.
