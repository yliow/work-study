  Using the fact that
  \[
    \int_{t=0}^{t=x} \frac{1}{n} t^{n - 1} \ dt = \frac{1}{n} x^n
  \]
  show that
  $f(x)$ satisfies an equation of the form
  \[
    (1 - x)f(x) - \int_{0}^{x} f(t) \ dt = T(0) + C \int_{0}^{x} \frac{1}{1 - t} \ dt + \frac{Dx}{1 - x}  
  \]
  where $C$ and $D$ are constants.
