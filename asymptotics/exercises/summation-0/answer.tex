$\sum_{i = 2}^5 i^2 = 2^2 + 3^2 + 4^2 + 5^2 = 54$ 

(b)
$\sum_{j = 0}^4 (2j) = 2\cdot 0 + 2 \cdot 1 + 2 \cdot 2 + 2 \cdot 3 + 2 \cdot 4 = 20$ 

        
(c)
$\sum_{k = 0}^4 (2k + 1) = 
(2\cdot 0 + 1) +
(2 \cdot 1 + 1) +
(2 \cdot 2 + 1) +
(2 \cdot 3 + 1) +
(2 \cdot 4 + 1) = 25$

(d)
$\sum_{\ell = 2}^5 3 \ell^2 =
3 \cdot 2^2 +
3 \cdot 3^2 +
3 \cdot 4^2 +
3 \cdot 5^2 = 162$
