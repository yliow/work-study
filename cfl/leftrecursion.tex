A production rule is
\defterm{left recursive}\tinysidebar{left recursive}
if it is of the form
\[
A \rightarrow A w
\]
where $A \in V$ and $w \in (V \cup T)^*$.
We also say the above production rule is a
\defterm{left recursion}\tinysidebar{left recursion}.

A \defterm{right recursion}\tinysidebar{left recursion}
is of course a production rule of the form
\[
A \rightarrow wA
\]
