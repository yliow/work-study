  Suppose you have a recurrence of the form
  \[
  a_n = f(n) + g(n) \sum_{i=0}^{n-1} h(i) a(i)
  \]
  for $n > n_0$ 
  where $f(n), g(n), h(n)$ are functions with $g(n) \neq 0$ for all $n > n_0$.
  Convert the recurrence to another of degree 1.
