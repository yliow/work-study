under the parabola from x = 2 to x = 7 is in fact exactly

\[(1 / 3)(7 * 7 * 7 -- 2 * 2 * 2)\]



Use your calculator (or C++) to compute this value and compare against

the value obtained by your program with n = 1000 rectangles.
