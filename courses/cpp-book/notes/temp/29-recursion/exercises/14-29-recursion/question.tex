\begin{ex}
What is the degree of this recursive definition:

$a(n) = 3a(n-1) + 4a(n-2) + 5a(n-3)$ if $n > 2$

$a(0) = 0$

$a(1) = 1$

$a(2) = 2$

What will happen if I only give you two base cases such as

$a(n) = 3a(n-1) + 4a(n-2) + 5a(n-3)$ if $n > 1$

$a(0) = 0$

$a(1) = 1$
\end{ex}
