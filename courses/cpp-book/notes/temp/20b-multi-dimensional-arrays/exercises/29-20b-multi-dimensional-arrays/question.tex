\begin{ex}
Given an integer array of size m-by-m, write a code segment to compute the sum of the entries on and above the main diagonal. For instance for the array:

\begin{align*}
1 & 2 & 3 \\
4 & 5 & 6 \\
7 & 8 & 9 \\
\end{align*}

The sum is 1 + 2 + 3 + 5 + 6 + 9.
\end{ex}
