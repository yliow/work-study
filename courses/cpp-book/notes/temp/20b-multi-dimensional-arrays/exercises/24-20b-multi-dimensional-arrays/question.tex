\begin{ex}
Declare an array of 5-by-5 characters and write loops to assignment values to the array so that it looks like this:

\begin{align*}
'X' & 'X' & 'X' & 'X' & 'X' \\
'X' & ' ' & ' ' & ' ' & 'X' \\
'X' & ' ' & ' ' & ' ' & 'X' \\
'X' & ' ' & ' ' & ' ' & 'X' \\
'X' & 'X' & 'X' & 'X' & 'X' \\
\end{longtable}

(This builds a wall for a 5-by-5 maze.) Rewrite the code so that it works for any size by using a constant for the size.
\end{ex}
