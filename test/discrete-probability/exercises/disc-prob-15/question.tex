  Following the standard way to solve a Bernoulli differential equation,
  let
  \[
    m(x) = e^{\int p(x) \ dx}
  \]
  Then $f(x)$ is given by
  \[
    f(x) = \frac{\int m(x) q(x) \ dx + E }{m(x)}
  \]
  where $E$ is a constant.
